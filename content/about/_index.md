Oxfordshire DIY Tool Hire is a small local side hustle run from Grove in Oxfordshire.

Instead of running a big anonymous warehouse, this is a friendly, practical service aimed at helping neighbours, homeowners and renters get jobs done without buying every tool outright.

Most of the tools are ones we use for our own DIY projects, so they are chosen carefully, looked after properly and supplied with clear instructions and safety guidance.
